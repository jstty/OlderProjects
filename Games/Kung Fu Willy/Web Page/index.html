Grab a shovel and dig in because this suckers taken off baby. Hello, if you could not all ready tell I am a full time Super Nerd and part time Game Programmer/Developer/Hell you name it.

My latest creation, Kung Fu Willy, valiantly forces man against man in a never ending slaughter to the death. The game takes place in a far way enchanted land filled with danger, chaos and mayhem. Your adventure begins on the verge of a horrific war the solders stand ready and willing to die for there master and king . . . .

Just kidding. Kung Fu Willy is a simple wanna-be fighting game. The character graphics are nothing to get excited about but the background is really cool. Kung Fu Willy is my first game and I am hoping some of you all will at least play a few round before dumping it from the hard drive.

On to more pressing matter. The game's backgrounds and midi files are randomly selected each time you play. It also has a neat but crappie CD play which is controlled directly by key touch on the keyboard. And finally we reach the end of our journey but the hidden(yea right) fatality(in no way relation to the #@*$ so called fatalities in a game we all know) is still to be found.
